Toyota Corolla GR 2023

To the delight of purists, the Toyota GR Corolla receives an optimized version of the GR Yaris' three-cylinder, 1.6-liter engine, tuned to develop a total of 300 hp and 273 lb-ft. Power is sent to all four wheels via a six-speed manual transmission there is no manual transmission option and a GR Four all wheel drive system, developed specifically for high-performance purposes.
